FEATURED ARTIST

Huh Yunjin


Huh Yunjin, also known as Jennifer Huh, is a member of the South Korean girl group LE SSERAFIM under Source Music. Born on October 8, 2001, in Seoul, South Korea, she grew up in the USA before moving back to pursue her dream of becoming a trainee. Huh Yunjin believes she can change the K-pop industry

She was revealed as the sixth and final member on April 9, 2022. The group debuted with the extended play Fearless on May 2, 2022; Huh was credited as a songwriter on the track "Blue Flame". On August 9, 2022, Huh released her first single "Raise Y_our Glass" to commemorate the 100th day of her debut with Le Sserafim. She co-wrote three songs for Le Sserafim's second EP, Antifragile: "Impurities", "No Celestial" and "Good Parts (When the Quality Is Bad but I Am)".



On Music
Huh released her second single "I ≠ Doll" on January 9, 2023, a rock-influenced track addressing the judgment she faces as an idol based solely on her appearance. Her third single, "Love You Twice" (피어나도록), dropped on March 14, 2023, accompanied by a music video depicting a fan inspired by an idol. "Blessing in Disguise," her fourth single released on August 14, 2023, addresses her return to South Korea with funk influences. On January 17, 2024, Huh featured on GroovyRoom's "Yes or No" with Crush, peaking at number 57 on the Melon chart. She later collaborated with Max on "Stupid In Love" and with J-Hope on "i don’t know" for his album released on March 29, 2024.
